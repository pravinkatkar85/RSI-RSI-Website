Company Profile


Real Strength Infrastructure, an ISO-9001:2015 Certified Company, is a well established, multi-faceted Engineering consultancy organization in India and serving the nation for more than 10 years. We are a leading Consulting Organisation specialised in Highways, Bridges and Structures, Traffic & Transportation, Buildings, Industrial Structure, Environmental Engineering and Rural Development Sector. Formed in the year 2011, RSI is a fast growing Engineering & Management consultancy firm providing entire range of solutions of technical & Project Management Consultancy in the area of infrastructure development works right from conceptualization to Project Commissioning.

Real Strength Infra is an integrated infrastructure development company. Over the years, we have built in-depth and extensive expertise in civil construction as well as consultancy. Backed by a highly skilled team of planners, designers, construction engineers and project managers, it has been our constant endeavour to offer a best value to our clients.

Real strength Infra was founded to bridge the gap between the rigorous demands of infrastructure development and the need for reliable quality service provider. With comprehensive services, we became the one-stop solution provider of highway consultancy and construction services.

We believe that we have held true to our founding principles throughout the years thanks to our leadership, approach, values, and goals. Our differences are our strengths, and we hope they make us the right choice for you.

Quality Focus

With big infrastructure Projects comes with great responsibility, we have an uncompromising policy towards quality. We shoulder complete responsibility for the intangible elements like effort, integrity and promise and provide best consultancy services to the client with high level of dedication and efficiency.
